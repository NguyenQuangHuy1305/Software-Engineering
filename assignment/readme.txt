button.py:
- the class Button, to init Button objects, and a method to draw the button and check if the button was clicked on
- 30 lines of codes
- Naming convention:
 + Function: use a lowercase word or words. Separate words by underscores
 + Variable: Use a lowercase single letter, word, or words. Separate words with underscores
 + Class: Start each word with a capital letter. Do not separate words with underscores
 + Module: Use a short, lowercase word or words. Separate words with underscores
 + Imported packages: Use a short, lowercase word or words. Do not separate words with underscores

- main game, containing the class Piece, to init a Tetris pieces, and other methods
- 759 lines of codes
- Naming convention: same as button.py
